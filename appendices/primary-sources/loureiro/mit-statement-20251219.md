# MIT Statement on Professor Nuno Loureiro

**Source:** MIT News, Massachusetts Institute of Technology
**URL:** https://news.mit.edu/2025/statement-professor-nuno-loureiro
**Date published:** December 19, 2025
**Date accessed:** April 20, 2026
**Tier:** T1 (institutional primary source)
**Confidence:** Confirmed

## Key Facts Extracted

- MIT expressed gratitude to law enforcement for identifying the suspect
- Confirmed the suspect was also responsible for the Brown University shooting
- Directed further inquiries to law enforcement and U.S. Attorney of Massachusetts
- MIT stated the community "continues to mourn and remember Nuno -- an incredible scientist, colleague, mentor, and friend"
- Indicated plans for future commemorative events

## Significance
This is MIT's official statement following the identification of suspect Claudio Manuel Neves Valente. It links the Loureiro murder to the Brown University shooting for the first time in an institutional source from MIT.
