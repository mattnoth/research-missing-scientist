## Summary

Riverside Research announced the election of Dr. Neil McCasland to its Board of Trustees, effective June 27, 2019. The release described McCasland as having retired from the U.S. Air Force as a Major General after a 34-year career, culminating as Commander of the Air Force Research Laboratory at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, where he led the USAF's $4 billion science and technology program.

John Giering, Chairman of the Board, was quoted praising McCasland's experience as AFRL commander and expertise in "emerging technology and intelligence programs" as assets to Riverside Research's technology roadmap and strategic growth.

Riverside Research is described as a nonprofit advancing scientific research for the U.S. government.
