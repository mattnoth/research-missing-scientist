# DOE / NNSA — Statement on Reports Related to Lab Employees

- **Source URL:** https://www.cbsnews.com/news/deaths-disappearances-scientists-staff-government-labs/
- **Access date:** 2026-04-20
- **Publisher/Author:** CBS News (quoting NNSA spokesperson)
- **Document type:** Agency official statement
- **Date of document:** April 2026

## Content

A spokeswoman for the U.S. Department of Energy's National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) stated to CBS News: "NNSA is aware of reports related to employees of our labs, plants, and sites and is looking into the matter."

Separately, Energy Secretary Chris Wright stated on Fox News Sunday (April 19, 2026): "A lot of the nuclear security scientists are in DOE. So yes, of course we are looking into this." When asked about findings, Wright said: "Too early to say about that. We haven't found anything alarming yet."
