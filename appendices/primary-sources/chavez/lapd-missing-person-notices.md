## Paraphrased Summary of May 12, 2025 Notice

The Los Alamos Police Department reported Anthony Chavez, 78, of Los Alamos as a missing person. He was reported missing on May 8, 2025. He is described as a white male, 5 feet 6 inches, approximately 135 pounds, and wears glasses. Family and friends stated the disappearance is out of character. LAPD said he is not believed to be endangered but asked the public for assistance. Contact: (505) 662-8222.

## Paraphrased Summary of May 19-20, 2025 Search Update

Deputy Chief James Rodriguez stated that officers were actively following every lead, reviewing evidence, and coordinating with multiple agencies and local businesses. Search efforts included: thorough searches of known residences, hiking of local trails, distribution of flyers to businesses, and review of multiple hours of surveillance footage. The LAPD emphasized that public tips are invaluable and urged anyone with relevant information to contact them.
