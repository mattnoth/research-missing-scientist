# Middlesex District Attorney Marian Ryan — Statement on Body Recovered from Lake Quannapowitt

- **Publisher/Author:** Town of Wakefield, MA (hosting DA Ryan's statement)
- **Document type:** District Attorney press release
- **Date of document:** 2026-03-17

## Content

District Attorney Marian Ryan and Wakefield Police Chief Steven Skory confirmed that a body was recovered from Lake Quannapowitt on the afternoon of March 17, 2026. Based on preliminary information including the victim's clothing, authorities believe the remains are those of Jason Thomas, a 45-year-old man reported missing on December 13, 2025.

Thomas left his residence on the evening of December 12, 2025. When he failed to return, his wife filed a missing person report with Wakefield Police early the following morning. Law enforcement conducted an extensive search using resources from the Northeastern Massachusetts Law Enforcement Council (NEMLEC), K-9 units, and drones.

Around 12:30 PM on March 17, a Wakefield Police detective discovered what appeared to be a body in the water near the frozen area of the lake. A drone was deployed to confirm the discovery, and police and fire personnel subsequently recovered the body.

The statement notes that "no foul play is suspected" based on preliminary information. The Office of the Chief Medical Examiner was to conduct an examination to formally identify the remains and determine the cause and manner of death.
