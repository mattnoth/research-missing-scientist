# LASD Statement — Acting Captain Ryan A. Vienna

**Source:** Crescenta Valley Weekly
**URL:** https://www.crescentavalleyweekly.com/news/07/03/2025/update-on-efforts-to-locate-missing-hiker-monica-reza/
**Date of Statement:** Week of June 29, 2025 (published July 3, 2025)
**Tier:** T1 (Law Enforcement official statement via local news)

## Key Excerpts (max 15 words each)

- Vienna: "An extraordinary search was initiated that involved our Los Angeles County Search and Rescue teams, numerous resources from throughout the state leveraging local, state and federal assets."
- "While today concludes our initial response phase of this operation, the investigation will continue by our Homicide Bureau: Missing Persons Unit."
- "Given this is an ongoing investigation, we would ask the public to avoid actively searching this area."
- "On behalf of the family, we are conveying their request for privacy at this time."

## Key Facts

- Initial response phase concluded June 29, 2025 (one week after disappearance).
- Search used forensic technology, photography, aerial resources, canine units, specialized tools.
- Case transferred to Homicide Bureau Missing Persons Unit.
- Family requested privacy through LASD.
- Contact: Det. Shannon Rincon, Det. Richie Sanchez, (323) 890-5500.

## Notes

This is the most detailed official LASD statement located. Vienna's statement confirms the scale of the search and the transfer to the Homicide Bureau, which handles both homicides and high-priority missing persons cases.
