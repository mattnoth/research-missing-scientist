## Content

Monica Jacinto Reza, 60, an aerospace engineer who worked at NASA JPL and Aerojet Rocketdyne, disappeared on June 22, 2025, while hiking with a friend near Mount Waterman in the Angeles National Forest. Her companion reported that they were roughly 30 feet apart; Reza smiled and waved, and moments later had vanished when the friend turned around.

An extensive multi-month search was conducted involving helicopters, radar, search dogs, and volunteers. The initial search phase concluded without finding any trace of Reza. She remains missing. Officials have not confirmed any connection between her disappearance and other scientist cases.

Reza co-invented Mondaloy, a nickel-based superalloy used in rocket engines, while working at Rocketdyne in the mid-1990s.
