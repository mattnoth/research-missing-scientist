## Content

Frank Werner Maiwald, aged 61, passed away on July 4, 2024, in Los Angeles, California. Born June 24, 1964, in Ratingen, Germany.

Maiwald dedicated many years at JPL, where he managed the development of the SBG-VSWIR instrument and had previously overseen successful delivery of two instruments for the AMR-C program. His roles included technical group supervisor. He contributed to projects including AMR/SWOT, COWVR, AMR/Jason 3, and HIFI. He had been at JPL since 1999 and worked on multiple projects tied to advanced satellite technology.

The cause of death was not included in the obituary and has never been made public. Officials confirmed that an autopsy was never performed. NASA and JPL declined to comment on his death when approached by media. No NASA/JPL press release or memorial statement was issued.
