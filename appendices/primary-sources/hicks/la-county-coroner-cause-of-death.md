## Content

The Los Angeles County Coroner listed the cause of death for Michael David Hicks as "arteriosclerotic cardiovascular disease" with "morbid obesity" described as a "significant condition." The manner of death was ruled "natural." Hicks died on July 30, 2023, at his residence in Sunland, California, at age 59.

Hicks worked at NASA JPL as a research scientist from 1998 to 2022, specializing in the physical properties of comets and asteroids. He served on the science teams of the DART Project, NEAT Project, Dawn Mission, and NASA Deep Space 1 Mission, and authored over 80 peer-reviewed papers. He held a PhD in Lunar and Planetary Science from the University of Arizona.
